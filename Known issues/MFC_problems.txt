After a reboot there might be a chance that the MOXI adapter resets back to rs-232 communication settings when it should be rs-485 2w.

1. Go to "Device Manager"
2. fully open up "Multi-port serial adapters"
    2.1. If you don't see this then check the connection of the mfc cable.
3. Right-click on "UPort 1150" and open "Properties"
4. Go to "Ports Configuration" tab
5. "Port Setting"
6. Change the Interface to "RS-485 2W"
7. "OK"
